WHO IS MOGUTOU?

蘑　菇　头

Mogutou (蘑菇头) is the single most-used emoticon character in Chinese internet history.

Born from the Baoman (暴漫) rage-comic universe in the early 2010s and later reborn as a stand-alone sticker pack on WeChat, the chubby mushroom-haired face with sparkling eyes and a wide-open laughing mouth quickly became the universal language of Chinese chatrooms — the way you say "lmao," "based," "speechless," "crying," and "wtf" all at once.

From group chats to Weibo to Douyin, Mogutou has been edited, remixed, and re-captioned more than almost any other character on the Chinese internet. He doesn't just survive trends — he is the trend.
